About Dr. Sudeep Neupane

Dr. Sudeep Neupane is an astrophysicist at the Max Planck Institute for Radio Astronomy in Bonn, Germany. He has expertise in observational astronomy, data analysis, and exploring the cosmos using advanced radio and submillimeter telescopes worldwide.

Originally from Nepal, he earned his Ph.D. in Astronomy from the University of Chile, leading multi-year observational projects with world-class facilities in the Atacama Desert.

His work has contributed to international collaborations and peer-reviewed publications, advancing our understanding of the universe. A complete list of his research outputs is available on his NASA ADS profile.

Research Interests

  • Radio (mm/sub-mm) Astronomy
